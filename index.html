About Me

A little bit about me

Over the last decade I've worked across almost every facet of data science—from causal experiments and building and deploying ML models to data science and analytics infrastructure.

One of the most important things I've learned is that for data science to have impact, it can't live only in a Jupyter notebook. It's about making the latest work actionable and useful, not just deployed.
